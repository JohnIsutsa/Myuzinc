FASHION

Fashion is one of the most thrilling, rewarding and exceptional event that MYUZINC like to work on. From the technical expertise, to promotion, to equipment, skills, project management and production, which MYUZINC have in abundance. MYUZINC creative team and designers help you to customise every detail of the fashion for a thrilling experience. Our involvement however is as flexible as you need it. We’re as comfortable working with subtle aesthetics as we are completely experiential theatre driven productions to make your show as memorable as possible. MYUZINC host some of most talented visual artists, lighting designers, interior designers, sound designers, who work together with your event line designers to detail, style, atmosphere and ethos you want to convey. We have mastered the design and build of immaculate set and catwalks to ensure perfect light for both press and film.

Our services include:
Lighting design: light distribution, even colour temperature and high colour rendition to ensure the best photos can be taken
Printed and LED Backdrop Projections
Rigging
Audio
Media services
Rails and interior décor
Customized Theme for the Event
Custom Ramp design & Hire Fashion Show benches and furniture
Content Distribution
Special Effects including:Kyrogenifex & Nitrogen
