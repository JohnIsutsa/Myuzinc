Nimeahidi Kenya.

NNimeahidi Festival is a platform, structure, network, community, environment and district for the stimulation, growth, promotion and creative use of a music aesthetic, sensibility and culture that draws from the vast, rich and diverse Eastern African realities and experiences for purposes of personal, community, national and regional transformation. Nimeahidi helps artists and local residents to sustain and develop cultural heritage and individual expression as well as provide a wide range of cultural experiences and opportunities to residents and visitors in urban spaces. The mandate of Nimeahidi is to enhance public value for the arts and revitalise individual and social responsibilities through creating public arts gallery, Music Performances, Halls of fame and erecting cultural & heritage monuments in urban spaces.
