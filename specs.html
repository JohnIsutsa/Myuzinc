Special Effects

We can provide visuals, 4D animations, projections and graphics for everything from branded prints to 3D animated projection on buildings. MYUZINC are one of the leaders in Architectural Mapping, which is the process of adding 3D animated effects to static structures like buildings or shows.

Kyrogenifex & Nitrogen

This special effect gives the audience a unique experience by adding to the already present aural & visual impact and taking the experience a step further by stimulating their tactile senses within the energy of a packed crowd. Imagine the audience being hit by the jets of freezing cold air!

Different Color Confetti & Streamers

Unlike the retail handheld "party poppers" or conventional fan blowers, our canons are powered by refrigerated CO2 liquid, giving a projectile distance of not less than 35 meters at an angle of 60 degrees, covering an area of over 150 sqm. We can provide customized confetti of any size, shapes, colors or logo cut outs.

Kyro-Man

Kyroman is a performer on stilts, with a sophisticated costume consisting of RGB, LEDs, Lasers, holding CO2 guns in his hands that releases chilling bursts of CO2 at temperatures below -90 degrees on the audience.

HOLOGRAPHIC uses a mixture of artistic theatre set design concepts, HD digital projection and 3D animations. The effect that is created shows live video and animated content floating around a presenter, event space or set. The effect is engaging, exciting and fresh. Presenters appear to interact with the content as a live hologram. HOLOGRAPHIC is an excellent example of technology and art merging; as well as tried and tested technologies being merged to create incredible audience experiences. HOLOGRAPHIC is the world's most innovative holographic presentation system, pioneered in the East Africa by MYUZINC. HOLOGRAPHIC is available for events throughout the world and MYUZINC team can provide just the proprietry system, or a complete production based around the HOLOGRAPHIC technology. The advantages of HOLOGRAPHICS are:
Uses minimal stage space.
Can work in almost any venue.
Does not require heavyweight rigging.
Does not need headroom above normal stage height.
